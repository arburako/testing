Our Story

ParkPlease launched it’s service in May 2012 for San Francisco’s Outside Lands Music Festival, held every year at Golden Gate Park. Our launch made city and regional headlines and helped over 180,000 concert goers find stress free parking.

Since then, ParkPlease has connected thousands of parking space owners to drivers looking for spaces and has successfully facilitated parking for numerous events including America’s Cup, Fleet Week, SF Giants Games and the Hardly Strictly Blue Grass Music Festival.

In two summer seasons of operation, ParkPlease has registered hundreds of parking spots in the San Francisco Bay Area and facilitated over $25K in revenue.

What We Built

An online community marketplace for parking, which allows anyone to list and rent parking spaces securely and conveniently. ParkPlease eliminates the annoyance and time wasted that occurs when searching for parking spaces, making the entire experience convenient, affordable and less overwhelming.

Technology

By guiding their online users through a straightforward step-by-step process, ParkPlease is able to simplify parking transactions.
